Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Overview

Debtdown is a personal debt payoff tracker. Your privacy is important. This policy explains what data Debtdown collects, how it is stored, and how it is used.

Data We Collect

Debtdown does not collect any personal data. All data you enter — including debt names, balances, interest rates, and payment history — is stored exclusively on your device and, if you have iCloud enabled, in your private iCloud account.

How Your Data Is Stored

  • On-device storage: Your data is stored locally on your iPhone using secure on-device storage (Keychain and UserDefaults).
  • iCloud sync: If you have iCloud enabled, your data may sync to your private iCloud account via Apple's CloudKit service. This data is only accessible to you and is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any contact information.
  • We do not collect financial account numbers or login credentials.
  • We do not use third-party analytics or tracking tools.
  • We do not share any data with third parties.
  • We do not sell data of any kind.

Crash Reporting

Debtdown uses Apple's built-in crash reporting, governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. Apple may collect anonymized diagnostic information when the app crashes. This data is not linked to your identity and is used only to improve app stability.

In-App Purchases

Debtdown offers a one-time in-app purchase ("Debtdown Pro"). Purchase transactions are processed entirely by Apple and governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to your payment information.

Notifications

If you enable payment reminders, Debtdown sends local notifications on your device. These notifications are generated entirely on-device and no notification data is transmitted to any server.

Children's Privacy

Debtdown is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
